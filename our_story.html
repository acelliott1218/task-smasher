Why Do All This?

Task smasher was started with on simple task in mind -- to use the time we've got doing things we love, instead of the things that keep us busy. Let's be honest; nobody, or at least very few, people love doing chores -- but when one of you's in medical school, and another working full-time, you've only got a few hours to yourself throughout the day. So our question: why spend those hours with busy-work?

Our Goal!

We know we're not going to solve every single issue, but we hope these four tasks we've outlined, which ended up saving us the most time, will do the same for you. Maybe it won't give you hours, but even minutes saved can be minutes spent with family and friends, instead of searching for keys, feeding the dog, and so on. We want to give you your life back, at least in this small way.

Why Trust Us?

We might be new, but for what we lack in brand-recognition, we make up for in passion. Again, medical school for one of us, and full-time work for another -- it's obvious time matters to us, and building this website took a lot of it! So, if time is so sparse for us, why would we go to all this trouble unless we knew it was worth it?

How You Can Help!

The four hacks we have right now are just our favorites -- but we know there's got to be thousands more out there. So, how can you help us out? It's simple! By going to our sign-up page you can send us your hacks. Who knows, they might be so good they dethrone one of our classic four!
